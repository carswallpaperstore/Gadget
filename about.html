Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading web development agency in India

Building Digital Excellence Since 2016

TechGuru India was founded in 2016 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make professional web development accessible to businesses of all sizes. What started as a small team of three passionate developers has grown into a full-service digital agency serving clients across India and beyond.

Our journey began when our founder, Rajesh Kumar, recognized the growing need for affordable, high-quality web solutions among small and medium businesses. He assembled a team of like-minded professionals who shared his commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.

Over the years, we've evolved from a basic web development shop to a comprehensive digital solutions provider. We've embraced new technologies, expanded our service offerings, and built lasting relationships with hundreds of satisfied clients.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted web development companies in India, known for our technical expertise, creative solutions, and unwavering commitment to client success.
